Mohsan Jameel

Hildesheim, Germany

I have joined Institute of Flight Guidance, Deutsches Zentrum für Luft- und Raumfahrt e.V. (DLR) as a Research Scientist with the major focus on Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence solutions for Air Traffic Management systems.

Previously, I was working as a Researcher in the field of Machine Learning and Data Science. The major focus of my work was on building machine learning models that scale with the increase in data and model complexity. As part of my work, I was actively working on industrial use-cases to apply machine learning models to improve and automate the decision processes. Before that, I was actively involved in research and development of software solutions for a high-performance computing environment, with a focus on improving system efficiency.
