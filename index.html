About

I am working as a Researcher in the field of Machine Learning and Data Scientist. The major focus of my work is on building machine learning models that scale with the increase in data and model complexity. As part of my work, I am actively working on industrial use-cases to apply machine learning models to improve and automate the decision processes. Previously, I was actively involved in research and development of software solutions for a high-performance computing environment, with a focus on improving system efficiency.
